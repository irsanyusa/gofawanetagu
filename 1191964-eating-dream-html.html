    You might be wondering about eating in the dream. What does it mean? Is it a good or bad sign? What if it just means that you need to skip your diet or change a lifestyle? In this article, we are going to discuss different interpretations of eating in the dream including in Christianity and other religions.

    What does it mean to be eating in the dream?

    While some people dream of food quite often, others would not dream of food at all. As a rule, dreaming of food is directly related to a person’s lifestyle or eating habits. If in conscious life you pay too little attention to the food you eat, eating problems will appear in the unconscious plane. Also, it is known that people who love eating, dream about food more often than those who eat only to maintain their healthy condition.

    On the other hand, dreaming of food may have some symbolic meaning. The way we eat in the dream, what we eat, how it looks like reflects our physical and psychological life in certain ways. When looking for an eating in the dream interpretation, give the answers to the following questions:

    • What are you doing in life to feed your soul and imagination?
    • How do you ensure you have enough energy to overcome the difficulties in your life?
    • Are you in touch with your body and soul?

    The process of eating in the dream

    The most important thing to determine is the process of eating in the dream. Tasty food is associated with a comfort. The best example is the food your mother prepared to show her care. If you are a dream eater, ask yourself do you like this food? Is it warm and tasty, nourishing and healthy, light and fresh?

    If we cannot recall the taste of food in the dream, we can describe its taste in general. Is it bitter or sweet? Was it disgusting or delicious? Then, we need to look for situations and events in our lives that the food might relate to.

    Meaning of eating in dreams

    • In general, food in dreams symbolizes well-being and prosperity. The amount of food we dream of, its quality might be a sign of the increase in wealth in the future or its decrease if the food is not tasty.
    • We can dream of tasty food if life we have been having some situations and problems difficult to solve.
    • Dreaming of a banquet can signify general abundance in life, not only in terms of material but emotional comfort. A food on a table might symbolize who our life looks like. If there is a lot of food, life is full of happiness that we are lacking something, both in physical and spiritual meaning. If you dream of the certain product, you might definitely need to include it in your diet. It might be dairy, fibre, meat, vitamin C, etc.

    • If you are dreaming of harmful and poisonous food, this might signify that some views and beliefs you stick to poison your life. It also might be behavior, lifestyle, a relationship you are engaged in, etc. Your subconsciousness is trying to tell you that you need to stop for a moment to consider your emotional life and identify whether it is healthy or not.
    • Those who are dieting dream of food more often than those who are sticking to a more or less balanced diet. Dreaming of food when dieting is not an expectable phenomenon as denying something can cause an opposite effect, a strong desire. Similarly, dreaming of food might be a result of starvation.
    • If you are dreaming of some food stuck in your throat, your mind is trying to tell you that there might be a situation, belief or view you cannot accept, you might find it “hard to swallow.” You might need to oversleep on it or get a piece of advice from some experienced person.
    • Dreaming of exotic foods might mean a lack of exploring new things, for example, hobbies, different cultures, etc. Your subconscious mind is trying to tell you that there are a lot of interesting things around, so you might need to travel somewhere or engage yourself in a new exciting activity, change some lifestyle habits, and so on.
    • If you are sharing food in the dream, your mind is possibly giving you a hint that you might start donating your possessions to others or sharing values and ideas you have with others. It concerns both physical and spiritual life. And vice versa, if you are offered food in a dream, your subconsciousness is trying to show you that you need to accept something valuable into your life.

    Eating in dreams, spiritual meaning

    From the spiritual point of view, eating food in the dream does not signify any spiritual attack, at least, the Bible does not say that eating in the dream is wrong.

    In Mark 16:18, the Bible says that even Christians who truly believe in God drink deadly poison, it cannot harm them:

    “They will pick up snakes with their hands; and when they drink deadly poison, it will not hurt them at all; they will place their hands on sick people, and they will get well”.

    When Peter was dreaming of food, he was extremely hungry. That’s why God used food to give him a revelation. Bible does not judge people who dream of food. The reason for it might consist in being hungry. If the person goes to bed with an empty stomach, the feeling of hunger in the dream might be unavoidable.
