    Much to the excitement of her fans, Cardi B is about to storm Nigeria with her fantastic music show. When exactly will we get to see Cardi B in Lagos? Where will she perform?

    A visit from such a huge superstar to Lagos means good things for the locals. The biggest city in Nigeria is a popular tourist attraction, and with the "Bodak Yellow" singer coming to perform her songs, it has a chance to improve its international reputation even more and attract more pop culture giants. Her Nigerian fans are extremely excited to see their favourite rap performer live.

    Cardi B in Lagos: when is she coming?

    Recently, the celebrity confirmed in an Instagram post that she will be attending shows in Africa after her multiple performances in America. The Cardi B tour in Africa will consist of two shows.

    The international superstar has her visit to Nigeria scheduled on December 7, 2019. She is coming to Africa to storm the Livespot X Festival, which is set to take place on December 7 in Lagos, Nigeria, and December 8 in Accra, Ghana.

    What is Livespot X Festival?

    The Livespot X Festival is a metropolitan music festival which is known for its vibrant atmosphere and regular surprises for its guests. It is a combination of great performances, quality music, and unforgettable experiences. It is inclusive and diverse, which means it is always open to people of all backgrounds as long as they are over the age of 18.

    This year, some of the most famous Nigerian celebrities are set to perform at the festival. This includes Shatta Wale, Sarkodie, Tiwa Savage, Becca, Darey, Patoranking, and Seyi Shay. You will also have an opportunity to see such acts as Medikal, Eno, DJ Vyrusky, DJ Loft, DJ Spinall, Kofi Mole, Eddy Blay, and Do2dtun. Of course, Cardi B will be the main headliner of the festival, but she is not the only reason to visit this diverse and entertaining event.

    How to buy tickets to the Livespot X Festival in Lagos

    If you want to purchase tickets to the Cardi B concert in Lagos and see many other famous singers gathered in the same place to perform their songs, there is an awesome opportunity to get your own passes to the festival.

    The tickets for the Livespot X Festival in Lagos are now available. You can book your tickets on the official Livespot Nation website.

    There are different categories which you can choose from.

    • Regular - N5,000 ($13.85)
    • VIP - N30,000 ($83)
    • Golden Circle (close to the stage, with beverages and refreshments) - N100,000 ($277)
    • A seat at the table - N350,000

    You can book or reserve the table by calling +234 809 222 2247 for Lagos.

    Who is Cardi B?

    The full name of this talented rapper and songwriter is Belcalis Marlenis Almanzar. She was born in New York City and eventually became an internet celebrity due to her viral videos and posts.

    Today, Cardi B songs are loved by many. Her single "Bodak Yellow" reached worldwide success. After that, she released more successful singles, such as "I Like It" and "Girls Like You," which was a collaboration with Maroon 5.

    Cardi B album "Invasion of Privacy," which came out in 2018 and was her debut work, became incredibly successful. It was certified triple platinum, broke multiple records and debuted as a number one album in the Billboard 200 chart. This made Cardi B the only female performer to win the Grammy Award in the Best Rap Album category as a solo artist. She beat such giants as Travis Scott, Nipsey Hussle, and Pusha T.

    If you are still hesitating on whether to attend the show, remember that it is a once in a lifetime opportunity to see Cardi B in Lagos on December 7. You will be able to hear her most famous hits live. So, do not waste any time and purchase your ticket for the Livespot X Festival as soon as possible.
