    When asked to clarify what he means by the statement that he was "always" a sex addict, Rocky responds plainly: "Like, I was horny," he says. "I ain't even had no sperm in my testicles yet, but I literally was just horny... I've been a sex addict for some time, yeah."

    When Rodriguez asks him if he's capable of a monogamous relationship, Rocky answers: "Yes. Of course."

    This isn't the first time Rocky has been frank about his sex life: in an profile with Esquire last year, he revealed that he regularly engages in threesomes, and had his "first orgy" at the age of 13. But in his interview with Rodriguez, he asserts that he is not embarrassed or ashamed.
