    We’ve all watched animated characters during our childhood. Yes, depending on when you were born, you might have seen different ones, but no child in the world is immune to the power of cartoons. Hidreley Diao, the creator of the Instagram account hidreley, came up with a very peculiar one to take us back to our childhood years using cartoons.

    Today, at Bright Side, we’d like to show you how the artist imagines what various characters from animated series and movies would look like if they were real human beings.

    1. Rapunzel and Flynn Rider from Tangled

    2. Shrek and Fiona from Shrek

    3. Naruto

    4. The Incredibles

    5. Aladdin and Princess Jasmin from Aladdin

    6. He-Man

    7. Peter and Lois Griffin from Family Man

    8. The Simpsons

    9. Ash Ketchum from Pokémon

    10. Princess Aurora and Maleficient from Sleeping Beauty

    11. Son Goku and Vegeta from Dragon Ball

    12. Charlie Brown from Snoopy

    13. The Madrigals from Encanto

    14. Snow White and The Evil Queen from Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs

    15. SpongeBob SquarePants

    16. Tiana and Prince Naveen from The Princess and the Toad

    17. Ariel and Ursula from The Little Mermaid

    18. Pinocchio

    What other cartoon characters do you wish you could see as if they were real people?
