    In her new Instagram videos, Dr. Pimple Popper tackles multiple lipomas all over her patients. These are not your typical lipomas, folks. These lipomas are wild and wily, and they definitely give Dr. Pimple Popper a hard time.

    The first set of lipomas are settled in on her patient's arm. There are multiple lumps on her upper and lower arm, but not for long. Dr. Pimple Popper captions this video: "Popping into the weekend like! TAG someone whos always excited for a good Saturday pop! #drpimplepopper"

    The video begins with Dr. Pimple Popper ready to squeeze. She's already created an opening and grabs the sides of the lipoma with her fingers. She squeezes and out pops a perfectly round lump of fat. Dr. Pimple knows this one looks good, she adds a slow motion replay for effect.

    The next lipoma is a much bigger challenge. Dr. Pimple Popper hints at the tough pop in her caption: "Sometimes all you can do is go piece by piece! Link in bio to watch this full exclusive #drpimplepopper"

    She slices into her patient's forehead to reach the lipoma, but this one will take more than a squeeze. (Yep, this lipoma grew right above her patient's eye.) She uses her tweezers and scissors to cut away at the lipoma. She grabs one piece and then another, until she finally has all the bits of the lipoma out of his head.

    Dr. Pimple Popper's fans had all kinds of creative ideas for this lipoma. One fan wrote: "The real Hell Boy " Another commenter added: "Stew meat?" One clever Popaholic commented: "Ouch, his brain is leaking out."

    You all know Dr. Pimple Popper wouldn't allow that to happen. Instead, she cleared away just the lipomas, even the stubborn ones.
