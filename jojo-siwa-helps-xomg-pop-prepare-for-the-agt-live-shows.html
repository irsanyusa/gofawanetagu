    JoJo Siwa’s girl group XOMG POP is preparing for the live shows of America’s Got Talent. Siwa is helping the group rehearse their routine ahead of Tuesday’s show, and she recently posted a TikTok in which she joins in on the performance.

    JoJo Siwa Performs with XOMG POP Ahead of AGT

    “My girl group challenged me to do their America’s Got Talent performance with them,” Siwa wrote on the TikTok video, adding, “Even though I choreographed it, they didn’t think I’d be able to do it.”

    In the fun clip, Siwa can be seen dancing around the rehearsal room with the group members while performing their song. It sounds like the group will be giving AGT itself a shout-out in their upcoming routine. We can’t wait to see how it looks on the big stage.

    Siwa asked fans how they thought she did with the performance. The clip has over 76,000 likes, so it’s safe to say people enjoyed it. In the video’s caption, Siwa wrote, “Do I secretly wanna be a memeber [sic] of @Team Siwa ….. yes.”

    SEE ALSO: MEET JOJO SIWA’S GIRL GROUP XOMG POP, WHO WERE THEY BEFORE ‘AGT’?

    Will the Group Earn America’s Vote This Week?

    XOMG POP was created by JoJo Siwa and her mom Jessalynn on the Peacock competition series Siwa’s Dance Pop Revolution. The group opened for Siwa on her D.R.E.A.M. tour. They’ve released the songs “Candy Hearts,” “Merry Go Round,” and “Secret Handshake.”

    In their audition for AGT Season 17, XOMG POP performed “Candy Hearts,” showing off colorful outfits and energetic dance moves. The judges praised their performance and gave them four “yes” votes to move on in the competition.

    The judges chose the group to move on to the live shows. They’ll perform this week for America’s vote. Will they make it to the Finals? Watch their performance this Tuesday night on NBC, and tune in on Wednesday to find out the results.
