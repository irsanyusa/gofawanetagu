    Jordyn Woods Loves Posting Bikini Pictures

    This isn’t the only bikini picture Jordyn has posted recently. On Earth Day, Jordyn thanked “Mama Earth” with a video of her inside of the ocean. Again she is wearing an orange bikini and we have to say orange is definitely her color. The internet went wild as they always do for Jordyn’s posts. The video now has over 1.4 million views and the comments are flooded with heart eye emojis.

    It seems like Jordyn is spending her quarantine reminiscing about trips she has taken in the past. Just before posting her video to show her appreciation for the Earth, she posted another bikini picture showing off her amazing figure. She is wearing a nude-colored bikini with matching earrings and looks like she in paradise with palm trees all around her. She captioned the post “imissyouandiloveyou” with a bunch of aquatic emojis.

    Fans think that the caption on the post refers to missing former bestie Kylie Jenner. “I think [she misses Kylie] because this photo was taken on her vacation with Kylie,” said one fan in an Instagram comment. The picture was reportedly taken during a vacation that Jordyn and Kylie went on together. But perhaps Jordyn is just saying that she misses the location and isn’t specifically referencing her old friendship with Kylie. A lot of the comments on this post said that Jordyn is the real view in the picture. She definitely can grab anyone’s attention.

    Jordyn’s Modeling Career Launched With A Little Help From The Kardashians

    Jordyn is no stranger to taking gorgeous pictures. She has previously modeled for Vogue and signed a contract with the Wilhelmina International Modeling agency. But she definitely had a little help launching her modeling career after being friends with Kylie. This prompted Khloe Kardashian to recruit Jordyn to model for her clothing line Good American.

    She has since been removed from the Good American site after kissing Khloe Kardashian’s baby daddy, Tristan Thompson. This caused a wedge between her relationship with the Kardashian-Jenner clan. However, it looks like Jordyn’s modeling career is still thriving especially after her bikini pics. She recently landed the cover of Rolling Out.

    She wrote in her caption that her “time is now.” She’s totally right considering she unleashed her newfound singing talent on national television and plans to pursue music professionally after her stint on The Masked Singer. We cannot wait to hear her album.
