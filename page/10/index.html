Newly released video showed 15 US Marines being detained during their formation in Camp Pendleton, San Diego, on July 25. The videos, which were first obtained by The San Diego Union-Tribune , showed the arrested Marines being handcuffed by Naval Criminal Investigative Service (NCIS) agents in front of their peers at parade-rest. Attorneys representing two of the Marines told The Union-Tribune that their clients's arrest was a violation of the Uniform Code of Military Justice, due to what they considered to be a pretrial punishment and unlawful command influence a term that refers to military leaders abusing their position by influencing the outcome of legal proceedings.
