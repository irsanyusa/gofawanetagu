The role of Abobaku in the Yoruba Royal Monarchy

21 May 2024, 00:00

The Abobaku title is a form of chieftaincy that, like kingship, is peculiar to some certain families. Abobaku lives and enjoys the same luxurious life as the king; whatever the king eats or drinks, he has the same. When the king is on official or royal duties and is needed to travel, whether outside the town or country, the Abobaku chief follows. Safe to say, whatever luxury the king gets, he gets the same; wherever the king goes, he follows.
