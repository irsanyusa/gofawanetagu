    When murmurs made their way around social media that Taylor Swift and Calvin Harris had broken up, both camps quickly moved to silence the rumor.

    After Radar Online reported the musical duo had split up after Harris supposedly received a risqué massage, Harris took to Twitter to deny the claims. Swift’s publicist, Tree Paine, also posted a tweet saying Radar’s report was untrue. While Swift herself has not weighed in on social media, People reports that a source close to the couple says the rumor is “absolutely not true” and pointed out that Harris has been favoriting Swift’s activities on Twitter and Instagram in recent days.

    I've said it before and I'm saying it again, do not believe Radar Online.

    — Tree Paine (@treepaine) October 12, 2015

    Don’t let the rumors get to you, Taylor, just shake it off.
