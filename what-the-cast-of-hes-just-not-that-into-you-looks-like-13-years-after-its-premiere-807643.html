    Watching a rom-com is the perfect plan for a weekend night. Among the endless list of movies from this genre, He’s Just Not That Into You stands out as one of the best. The film revolves around the story of different characters who experience relationship problems. Even if it lasts 2 hours, its witty humor and, above all, its sweet romance make time go by superfast.

    It’s been more than a decade since this beautiful movie was released, and Bright Side wanted to show you what the cast looks like today.

    1. Ben Affleck

    Ben Affleck was one of the leading men in the film. He played Neil, who does not believe in marriage but is deeply in love with his girlfriend, Beth. In 2014, the actor appeared in Gone Girl, also, since 2016, he has played Batman in various films. Most recently, Ben stared in Deep Water, a thriller that received positive reviews.

    2. Jennifer Aniston

    Jennifer plays Beth, who has been in a relationship with Neil for years but doesn’t want to get married. Recently, the actress participated in films such as Dumplin and Murder Mystery. She also returned to television with a leading role in the series The Morning Show, for which she received several award nominations. We can’t forget her appearance in Friends: The Reunion.

    3. Bradley Cooper

    Bradley plays Ben, a married man who starts an affair. Subsequently, he was part of other hits such as Silver Linings Playbook and American Hustle and even voiced Rocket Raccoon in the Marvel Universe. He also starred in A Star Is Born, which earned him his fourth nomination for Best Actor at the Oscars.

    4. Jennifer Connelly

    Jennifer played Janine, Ben’s wife. She’s struggling to save their marriage, but she files for divorce by the end of the movie. More recently, we could see her in the dystopian series Snowpiecer and in May 2022, she will star in the second installment of Top Gun.

    5. Scarlett Johansson

    Scarlett played Anna, the third wheel in Ben and Jeanine’s marriage. Another of the actress’ well-known roles is that of Black Widow. This features several Marvel movies. She also starred in several acclaimed films, such as Lucy, A Marriage Story, and Jojo Rabbit.

    6. Justin Long

    Justin was another of the film’s leading men. He played Alex, a bar owner who forms a friendship with Gigi and eventually falls in love with her. Subsequently, Justin appeared in films such as Veronica Mars and Lady of the Manor. He also voiced Kevin in the F is for Family animated series.

    7. Ginnifer Goodwin

    Ginnifer landed the role of the dreamy Gigi, a not-so-lucky girl when it comes to love. That’s until she meets Alex, of course. After starring in this romantic comedy, the actress became popular for playing Snow White in the series Once Upon a Time. She also voiced Judy for the animated film Zootopia.

    8. Drew Barrymore

    Drew is one of the queens of romantic comedies, without a doubt. In this case, she played the role of Mary, a die-hard romantic who eventually finds love. Since then, Barrymore has worked in movies like Blended and Miss You Already. In 2020, she launched her talk show; in 2021, she announced that she would take a break from acting.

    9. Kevin Connolly

    Kevin plays the character of Conor, a man in love with a friend. However, by the movie’s end, he realizes his true love is Mary. While shooting He’s Just Not That Into You, he also starred in Entourage. Connolly also appeared in Friends with Better Lives. In 2018, he directed Gotti.

    10. Busy Philipps

    Busy had a small part in the film as Kelli Ann, a girl that works in Alex’s bar. After that, she appeared in movies like The Gift or I Feel Pretty. She also had her own talk show, called Busy Tonight. Currently, she stars in the musical comedy series Girls5eva.

    What do you think is the best romantic comedy of all time? Tell us in the comments!
